# Spell Checker
Check text for spelling and grammar errors.

## How It Works
The tool analyzes your text and suggests corrections for:
- Spelling mistakes
- Grammar issues
- Punctuation errors
- Style improvements

## Supported Languages
- English (US/UK)
- French
- German
- Spanish

## Privacy
All text processing happens locally. No data is sent to external servers.
Your text is never stored or logged.

## Usage
Paste your text and the tool will highlight issues with suggested fixes.
Accept or reject each suggestion individually.
